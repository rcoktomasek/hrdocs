Scoring Guide
▾
Exceeded
Consistently goes above and beyond. Delivers results that surpass expectations, takes initiative without prompting, and positively influences others. Reserve for truly standout performance — typically 15–25% of ratings.
Met
Reliably meets the requirements of the role. Completes responsibilities on time, follows processes, and meets quality standards. This is strong, solid performance — expect this to be the most common rating (50–65%).
Not Met
Falls short of what the role requires. There are gaps in quality, timeliness, or consistency that need to be addressed through coaching or a formal improvement plan. Typically 10–20% of ratings.
N/A
Not applicable to this role or too early to assess. Use sparingly — only when the criteria genuinely doesn't apply (e.g., Leadership for a non-supervisory role).
